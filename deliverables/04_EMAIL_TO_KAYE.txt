A few items that may surface on review:

- LSH placement. The 2.5% Life Safety and Health allowance is a Marine Corps FSRM program adder (NAVFAC 11010.44E CH-1 / MCO 11000.12). The May 2024 manual is silent on a Block 9 line position for LSH. The draft above places it under Supporting Facilities as a Special Construction Features sub-line, anchored on Section 3.1 sequencing item 1 and the Section 4.9 definition of items "built into or part of the facility." If a different placement is preferred, the dollar value does not change.

- SIOH 8.0%. OCONUS FSRM customer-directed for MCIPAC G-F. Falls between the standard 7.3% OCONUS rate and the 9.0% Remote OCONUS rate per DASD-Con direction. Applied uniformly across all five buildings.

- 6.0% Design and 4.0% CM. NAVFAC agency-directed program adders. Authority NAVFAC 11010.44E CH-1.
