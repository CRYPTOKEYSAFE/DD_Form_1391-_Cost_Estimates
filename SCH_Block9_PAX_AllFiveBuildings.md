## Standing PAX Entry Rules (All Five Buildings)

1. One UNIFORMAT code per child line. PAX rejects combined codes.
2. All amounts in $000s to 3 decimal places (except SCH-3213 which uses whole $000s as entered).
3. SIOH: 8.0% OCONUS FSRM Customer-Directed (not 7.3% — that is the DCA standard rate; 8.0% is the FSRM customer-directed rate applied to all five Schwab buildings).
4. Contingency: 10.0% across all five buildings.
5. DB Design: 4.0% applied after Total Funded, not before.
6. LSH is calculated on the full AE PRV (entire building GSF), not the scoped area.
7. Duplicate UNIFORMAT codes across separate child lines are acceptable and correct. Do not consolidate scope groups that have independent cost bases.
8. Yellow-flagged items should be entered as shown with a note in the description field signaling the open item — do not withhold them from PAX pending resolution.
